## 🌏 About Megital

**Megital** is a platform designed to build the **digital identity layer for the modern internet**.

In today’s web, people exist across many platforms — social media, portfolios, messaging apps, communities — yet there is no true **center of identity**.

Megital aims to solve this by providing a system where individuals can create and manage their **digital presence through a flexible ecosystem of identity products.**

Our goal is simple:

> Give people the power to **own and shape their identity online.**

---

## 🚀 Core Products

Megital is not just a single application — it is an **ecosystem**.

### 🧑 Identity Pages

Customizable digital identity pages that represent individuals, creators, and professionals.

Users can organize links, content, and metadata to create a modern online presence.

---

### 🎨 Template System

A flexible template system that allows identities to be presented with different designs and structures.

This allows users to express themselves through **custom layouts and experiences.**

---

### 🔗 Identity Hub

A central location for organizing and sharing everything that represents a person's digital presence.

---

## 🔮 Future Products

Megital is designed as a **growing platform**.

### 💌 Digital Invitations

Create beautiful invitations for events, weddings, meetups, and celebrations — fully digital and shareable.

### 🌐 Identity Network

A discovery layer where digital identities can connect and interact.
